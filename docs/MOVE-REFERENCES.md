# Move references and implementation notes

Reviewed 2026-07-23. These sources guide recognizable support, direction, and
momentum; Kaki-Dance deliberately compresses anatomy and timing for plush
readability.

Foundation references:

- [WDSF overview](https://www.worlddancesport.org/News/Everything-you-need-to-know-about-Breaking-3459)
  for the four core components and five judging categories.
- [Red Bull basic elements](https://www.redbull.com/au-en/understand-the-basic-elements-of-breaking)
  for toprock, go-down, footwork, freeze, power, and transition vocabulary.
- [Red Bull Break Advice fundamentals](https://www.redbull.com/ca-en/break-advice-fundamentals)
  for six-step, baby-freeze, and windmill teaching references.
- [The Breaks encyclopedia](https://thebreaks.org/toc.html), CC BY 4.0, for
  mechanics and historical naming.

## Freezes

| Move | Movement reference | Kaki-Dance interpretation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Baby Freeze | [The Breaks: Baby](https://thebreaks.org/articles/baby.html) | Lift-baby-inspired plush variant: two planted paws, elbow/hoodie compression, horizontal head mass, and active balance |
| Chair Freeze | [The Breaks: Chair](https://thebreaks.org/articles/chair.html) | Two-hand plush chair variant emphasizing side-waist stab, asymmetric legs, and a narrow support region |
| Turtle Freeze | [The Breaks: Turtle](https://thebreaks.org/articles/turtle.html) | Two paws under the abdomen, compressed hoodie mass, and COM correction over the hand span |
| Headstand Freeze | [The Breaks: Headstand](https://thebreaks.org/articles/headstand.html) | Tripod support from head and both paws, with visible wobble before failure |

The freeze score also follows the documented musical role of a
[freeze](https://thebreaks.org/articles/freeze.html): the entry, surrounding
transition, direction, and beat placement matter, not only the held pose.

## Validation notes

- All contacts are tested at 101 phases in normal authored direction for
  KittyKaki and Soder; mirroring has a separate limb/anchor test.
- Current worst residual is below `0.01` logical pixel.
- Power loops retain direction and momentum and require player input to extend.
- Freezes use the declared support span, projected COM, stamina, difficulty,
  incoming momentum, and stick correction.
- No reference video, illustration, or sprite is shipped as a runtime move
  asset.
